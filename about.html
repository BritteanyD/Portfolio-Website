Get to Know Me

As someone who has embarked on the rewarding journey of being a self taught software engineer, I am immensely proud of how far I've come. Every step of this journey, from mastering the basics to tackling more complex concepts, has been an enjoyable experience filled with countless learning moments. I find joy in the smallest victories, whether it's aligning an element or solving a tricky bug successfully. Overall, I approach coding with the same passion and dedication that I bring to every aspect of my life. I am excited to continue growing, learning, and exploring new opportunities in this space.

When I'm not coding I enjoy spending time with my cat, socializing with friends, traveling, and handcrafting body butters for my small business.

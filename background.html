Background

My background as an educator has provided me with invaluable skills in communication, problem-solving, and adaptability, all of which have seamlessly transitioned into my coding journey. Similarly, my experiences working in the fine dining industry have taught me the importance of attention to detail, creativity, and delivering exceptional experiences. I see the overlap between these seemingly disparate worlds, finding parallels in the artistry of crafting a perfect dish and the craftsmanship of writing clean, elegant code.
